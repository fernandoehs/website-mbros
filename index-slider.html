About
a passion


Photography is an opinion, a point of view that we use to describe the conflicts of the human being, further telling those stories we can freeze them as alive forever through time. But also it is a fantasy that give us the experience to dream through shape and colors. Photography make us think, make us smile, make us dream and can even make us cry. In a nutshell, photography tells stories. Miguel Angel Rada (Caracas, 1982), is a Photographer and Engineer based in Miami. "I was influenced by art since I was a child, I grew up in the middle of the artwork of Carlos Cruz Diez ,the movement of making colors flow through geometry and hundred rythyms. Photography gave me the freedom to create and show the people creations, also the need to tell what happens"
